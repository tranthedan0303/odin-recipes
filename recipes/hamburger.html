Best Hamburger Ever

This is the best hamburger you'll ever taste!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the patties for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your desired doneness.
  4. Place the patties on the bottom half of the buns.
  5. Add lettuce, tomato, and cheese on top of the patties.
  6. Place the top half of the buns on top.
