Shoyu Chicken

Japanese Shoyu Chicken

This is a delicious Japanese-style chicken dish with a savory shoyu (soy sauce) glaze.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 1 tablespoon sake
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, mirin, sake, sugar, garlic, and ginger to make the shoyu sauce.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken thighs to the skillet and cook for 5-6 minutes on each side, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Pour the shoyu sauce over the chicken in the skillet and reduce the heat to medium-low.
  5. Simmer the chicken in the sauce for 5-7 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened and coats the chicken.
  6. Serve the shoyu chicken over steamed rice and garnish with chopped green onions or sesame seeds if desired.
