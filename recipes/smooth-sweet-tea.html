Refreshing Smooth Sweet Tea

This is a refreshing and smooth sweet tea recipe that is perfect for hot summer days.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 4 black tea bags
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. Remove the saucepan from heat and add the tea bags. Let them steep for about 5 minutes.
  3. Remove the tea bags and stir in the sugar until it is completely dissolved.
  4. Stir in the lemon juice.
  5. Let the tea cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until chilled.
  6. Serve the sweet tea over ice cubes and garnish with lemon slices if desired.
